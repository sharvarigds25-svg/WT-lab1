Inspiration & Legacy

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. Known as the "Missile Man of India", he played a key role in India's space and missile programs.

He inspired millions through his vision, humility, and dedication to education and innovation. His books and speeches continue to motivate students across the world.

Achievements

  • Bharat Ratna Award
  • Key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • President of India (2002–2007)
